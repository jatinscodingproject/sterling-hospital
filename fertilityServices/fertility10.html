PCOS Clinic

At Sterling Orthopaedic Hospital and IVF Centre, we specialize in the treatment of Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS), also known as PCOD (Polycystic Ovarian Disease). PCOS is a common hormonal disorder among women of reproductive age, characterized by irregular periods, ovarian cysts, hormonal imbalances, and fertility challenges.

Our dedicated team of doctors and specialists at the PCOD Clinic offers comprehensive care for women dealing with PCOS. We provide personalized treatment plans that may include lifestyle modifications, medication management, hormonal therapies, and fertility interventions. Our goal is to help you manage your PCOS symptoms effectively and improve your chances of conceiving naturally or through assisted reproductive techniques.
