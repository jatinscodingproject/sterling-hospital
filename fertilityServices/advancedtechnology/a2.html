Endometrial Receptivity Array (ERA)

Advanced Fertility Treatment in Virar & Mumbai

What is ERA?

The Endometrial Receptivity Array (ERA) is a diagnostic test that assesses the receptivity of the endometrial lining (the lining of the uterus) to optimize the timing of embryo transfer during IVF.

How ERA Works
  1. Biopsy Collection: A small biopsy of the endometrial lining is taken during a typical menstrual cycle. This procedure is performed in a clinic with minimal discomfort.
  2. Laboratory Analysis: The biopsy sample is analyzed in a specialized laboratory using advanced molecular techniques to assess gene expression patterns associated with endometrial receptivity.
  3. Results and Recommendations: Test results provide information about the optimal timing for embryo transfer. Based on these results, your fertility specialist will tailor the timing of your IVF cycle to improve implantation success.
Benefits of ERA
  • Increased Implantation Success: Identifies the most receptive phase of the endometrium, enhancing embryo implantation chances.
  • Personalized Treatment: Allows for a more personalized and effective IVF treatment plan by determining the ideal timing for embryo transfer.
  • Reduced Risk of Failed Cycles: Helps reduce the risk of failed IVF cycles by optimizing the timing of embryo transfer.
Who Should Consider ERA?

The Endometrial Receptivity Array (ERA) is a revolutionary diagnostic test designed to enhance the success of In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) by optimizing the timing of embryo transfer. By assessing the receptivity of the endometrial lining, ERA ensures that embryos are transferred during the most favorable window for implantation.
