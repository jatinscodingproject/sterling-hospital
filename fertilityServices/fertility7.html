Egg Freezing

Advanced Fertility Treatment in Virar & Mumbai

Egg Freezing

Egg freezing, also known as oocyte cryopreservation, is a revolutionary procedure that allows women to preserve their fertility by freezing and storing their eggs. This advanced technique is especially beneficial for women who wish to delay childbirth due to personal, medical, or professional reasons.

The Process of Egg Freezing
Consultation and Assessment

The journey begins with a thorough consultation with a fertility specialist. During this visit, a comprehensive assessment of the woman's overall health, reproductive health, and fertility status is conducted. This may include blood tests and ultrasound examinations to evaluate ovarian reserve and hormone levels.

Ovarian Stimulation

To maximize the number of eggs retrieved, the woman undergoes a process called ovarian stimulation. This involves administering hormone injections over a period of 10-14 days to stimulate the ovaries to produce multiple mature eggs in one cycle. Regular monitoring through blood tests and ultrasounds ensures the optimal response to the medication.

Egg Retrieval

Once the eggs are mature, they are retrieved from the ovaries using a minimally invasive procedure called transvaginal ultrasound aspiration. This procedure is performed under sedation or anesthesia to ensure comfort. A thin needle is guided through the vaginal wall into the ovaries to collect the eggs.

Freezing and Storage

The retrieved eggs are then rapidly frozen using a method called vitrification. This process prevents the formation of ice crystals, which can damage the eggs. The vitrified eggs are stored in liquid nitrogen at extremely low temperatures, where they can remain viable for many years.

Future Use

When the woman is ready to conceive, the frozen eggs are thawed and fertilized with sperm in a laboratory using in vitro fertilization (IVF) techniques. The resulting embryos are cultured for a few days before being transferred to the woman's uterus, where they can implant and develop into a pregnancy.

Advantages of Egg Freezing
  • Flexibility and Control: Egg freezing provides women with the flexibility to plan their family at a time that is right for them, without the pressure of their biological clock.
  • Medical Preservation: Women facing medical treatments that may affect their fertility, such as chemotherapy, can preserve their eggs before starting their treatment.
  • Career and Personal Goals: Women can focus on their career, education, or other personal goals without compromising their future fertility.
