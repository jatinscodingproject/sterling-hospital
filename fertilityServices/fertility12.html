About Hysteroscopy

At Sterling Orthopaedic Hospital and IVF Centre, we offer advanced hysteroscopy services, a minimally invasive procedure used to diagnose and treat uterine conditions that can affect fertility. Hysteroscopy involves the use of a thin, lighted tube called a hysteroscope, which is inserted through the vagina and cervix into the uterus to provide a clear view of the uterine cavity.

Our skilled team of doctors and specialists utilize the latest hysteroscopic techniques to address issues such as uterine polyps, fibroids, adhesions, and congenital abnormalities. This procedure not only helps in diagnosing the underlying causes of infertility but also allows for therapeutic interventions to correct these conditions. Hysteroscopy offers minimal discomfort and faster recovery times compared to traditional surgical methods. Trust us to provide you with comprehensive and compassionate care, guiding you towards achieving your reproductive goals with our state-of-the-art hysteroscopy services.
