About Us

Welcome to Sterling Orthopaedic and IVF Centre, where excellence in healthcare meets compassionate patient care. Nestled in the heart of Virar, our state-of-the-art facility offers unparalleled services in orthopedics, spine surgery, and fertility treatments. Our commitment is to provide personalized care with the latest advancements in medical technology, ensuring a holistic healing experience.

+ Our Mission

To deliver high quality, accessible, affordable, and reliable healthcare to our community.

+ Our Vision

To showcase to the world the best in patient care, surgical expertise, academics, medical ethics, and state-of-the-art technology. To be a center which will be the pride of our country.

+ Our Values
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence. We want to be inspirational. We challenge ourselves to push boundaries and offer the very best care in world-class facilities.
  • Empathy: We empathize with every single patient and their every need, fear, and hope. We understand their pain and we will do our very best to alleviate it.
  • Ethics: Our excellence rests on the bedrock of our ethics. Our first and only duty is to the patient's well-being, overriding any other consideration.
+ World-Class Facilities

Our center boasts modern facilities designed to provide a seamless and comfortable patient experience. Our infrastructure includes:

  • State-of-the-art operation theatres with HVAC system (air handling units) and laminar air flow systems for optimal sterilization
  • Advanced modular IVF lab with latest equipments
  • Orthoscannogram (first time in Virar)
  • Advanced C-Arm system and Digital X-ray (full body)
  • 24/7 accidents and emergency services
  • 24/7 ambulance services
  • Spacious waiting area with wheelchair assistance
  • Out patient department (OPD)
  • An in-house pharmacy
  • An in-house pathology lab
  • Deluxe and super deluxe spacious rooms with stunning views of Jeevdani Mandir, offering a serene and picturesque setting for our patients
+ Pleasant Ambiance and Healing Atmosphere

We understand that a healing environment extends beyond medical care. Our hospital is designed to offer a serene and pleasant ambiance that promotes healing and well-being. From the moment you step into our facility, you will be welcomed by a warm and inviting atmosphere, with comfortable waiting areas, soothing decor, and a friendly, attentive staff ready to assist you.

+ Meet Our Founders
Dr. Sachin Zambare - Orthopedic and Spine Surgeon

Dr. Sachin Zambare, an esteemed orthopedic surgeon, co-founded Sterling Orthopaedic and IVF Centre with a vision to revolutionize orthopedic care. With specialized training from top experts in Europe, including Spain, Switzerland, and Hungary, Dr. Sachin excels in joint replacement and spine surgeries. His expertise is complemented by his warm aura and reassuring smile, which instantly put patients at ease. Many find that half their worries vanish upon seeing him, a testament to his compassionate nature and professional excellence.

Leading the spine surgery department, Dr. Sachin combines precision and advanced techniques to restore mobility and enhance the quality of life for his patients. His dedication to advancing orthopedic treatment makes him a beacon of hope for those suffering from musculoskeletal issues.

Dr. Renu Singh - IVF Specialist

Dr. Renu Singh, a revered IVF specialist, co-founded our center with a passion for helping couples achieve their dreams of parenthood. Her extensive knowledge in reproductive medicine and gynecological laparoscopy has transformed countless lives. Dr. Renu's empathetic approach, coupled with her commitment to excellence, has established her as a trusted and beloved figure in fertility care.

Dr. Renu’s holistic and patient-centric approach ensures that each journey to parenthood is supported with the utmost care, compassion, and expertise.
