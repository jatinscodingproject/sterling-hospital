Under his leadership, our spine surgery department continuously strives to offer the best possible care to our patients.

Our Comprehensive Approach to Spine Care

Understanding the impact spinal conditions can have on a person's life, we provide a holistic approach to spine care. Our services encompass accurate diagnosis, personalized treatment plans, and a multidisciplinary team to support patients throughout their recovery journey. Whether it's a minor condition or a complex spinal disorder, we are equipped to offer both surgical and non-surgical solutions tailored to each patient’s needs.

Our Spine Surgery Services
  • Minimally Invasive Spine Surgery

    We specialize in minimally invasive techniques that reduce recovery time, minimize scarring, and decrease postoperative pain. These procedures include microdiscectomy and spinal decompression.

  • Spinal Decompression and Fusion

    For conditions such as herniated discs, spinal stenosis, and spondylolisthesis, we offer spinal decompression and fusion surgeries to alleviate pressure on the spinal cord and nerves, ensuring stability and improved function.

  • Disc Replacement Surgery

    As an alternative to traditional spinal fusion, disc replacement surgery helps preserve motion at the affected spinal segment, providing pain relief while maintaining mobility.

  • Spinal Deformity Correction

    We treat spinal deformities like scoliosis, kyphosis, and lordosis through advanced surgical techniques tailored to individual patient needs.

  • Spinal Trauma and Fracture Management

    Our team is skilled in managing spinal traumas and fractures, offering both surgical and non-surgical solutions to ensure the best possible outcomes.

  • Non-Surgical Spine Care

    We provide comprehensive non-surgical treatments, including physical therapy, pain management, and lifestyle modifications to support spinal health and enhance quality of life.
