Joint Replacement

Advanced Solutions for Joint Pain and Mobility Issues

Joint Replacement Services at Sterling Orthopaedic and IVF Centre

At Sterling Orthopaedic and IVF Centre, we are committed to providing exceptional joint replacement services to help you regain mobility and improve your quality of life.

Comprehensive Care and Advanced Techniques
1. Total Knee Replacement (TKR):

Our Total Knee Replacement procedures are designed to relieve pain and restore function in patients suffering from severe arthritis or knee damage. Using advanced surgical techniques and high-quality prosthetic implants, we ensure that patients receive the best possible care and achieve optimal recovery.

2. Total Hip Replacement (THR):

For patients experiencing debilitating hip pain due to arthritis, fractures, or other conditions, our Total Hip Replacement services offer a reliable solution. Our surgeons utilize minimally invasive techniques to reduce recovery time and improve surgical outcomes, ensuring that patients can return to their daily activities with greater ease.

3. Shoulder Replacement:

Shoulder replacement surgery at Sterling Orthopaedic and IVF Centre is tailored to address severe shoulder arthritis or complex fractures. Our team uses the latest prosthetic designs and surgical methods to provide patients with improved shoulder function and pain relief.

4. Revision Joint Replacement:

We also specialize in revision joint replacement surgeries for patients who have previously undergone joint replacement but are experiencing complications or implant failures. Our expertise in this area allows us to offer effective solutions and restore joint function.

Patient-Centered Approach

At Sterling Orthopaedic and IVF Centre, we prioritize patient education and engagement throughout the treatment process. From the initial consultation to post-operative care, our team provides comprehensive support and guidance to ensure that patients are well-informed and comfortable with their treatment plan.

Preoperative Assessment and Planning:

Every patient undergoes a thorough preoperative assessment to determine the best course of action. This includes detailed imaging studies, physical evaluations, and consultations to discuss the surgical plan and expected outcomes.

Customized Rehabilitation Programs:

Post-surgery, we offer tailored rehabilitation programs designed to aid in a swift and successful recovery. Our physiotherapists work closely with patients to develop personalized exercise regimens that enhance mobility, strengthen muscles, and promote healing.

State-of-the-Art Facilities:

Our operation theatres are equipped with the latest surgical instruments and air handling units with laminar flow systems to maintain a sterile environment and minimize the risk of infection. Additionally, our 24-hour emergency services and in-house pharmacy ensure that patients receive continuous care and support.

Commitment to Excellence

Sterling Orthopaedic and IVF Centre is dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of medical care. Our ongoing commitment to excellence, combined with our advanced facilities and skilled team, ensures that patients receive the best possible treatment for joint replacement surgeries.

Whether you are dealing with chronic pain or mobility issues, our joint replacement services are designed to help you get back to enjoying an active and healthy lifestyle. Contact us today to learn more about how we can assist you on your journey to recovery.
