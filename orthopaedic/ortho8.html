About Hand & Microvascular Surgeries

Sterling Orthopaedic Hospital and IVF Centre specializes in providing advanced surgical treatments for hand and microvascular conditions. Our team of skilled surgeons and medical professionals is dedicated to restoring hand function, treating injuries, and managing complex microvascular disorders.

We offer a range of surgical procedures for hand conditions such as fractures, nerve injuries, tendon repairs, and joint disorders. Our microvascular surgery services include replantation, free tissue transfer, and vascular reconstruction to restore blood flow and function in delicate tissues.

Whether you have experienced a hand injury or require microvascular surgery for a complex condition, trust Sterling Orthopaedic Hospital and IVF Centre are for expert care and successful outcomes. We prioritize precision, safety, and patient satisfaction in every surgical procedure we perform.
