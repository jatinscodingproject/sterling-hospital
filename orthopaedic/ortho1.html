Orthopaedic and Trauma Care

At Sterling Orthopaedic and IVF Centre, we offer specialized orthopaedic and trauma care led by Dr. Sachin Zambare. Our state-of-the-art facility in Virar is dedicated to providing exceptional care for patients suffering from musculoskeletal injuries and conditions. With a focus on advanced treatment methods and patient-centered care, we aim to restore mobility, alleviate pain, and enhance the overall quality of life for our patients.

Our Services
  • Comprehensive Trauma Care
    • 24/7 Emergency Services: We provide round-the-clock emergency care to ensure that patients receive immediate attention for acute injuries. Our emergency department is equipped with the latest technology and staffed by skilled professionals ready to handle all types of trauma cases.
    • Acute and Chronic Injury Management: Our team is proficient in managing both acute injuries, such as fractures and dislocations, and chronic conditions resulting from past trauma.
  • Orthopaedic Surgery
    • Fracture Treatment: We specialize in the surgical and non-surgical treatment of fractures, utilizing advanced techniques to ensure optimal healing and recovery.
    • Joint Reconstruction and Replacement: Our expertise includes joint reconstruction and replacement surgeries, aimed at restoring function and reducing pain for patients with severe joint damage.
  • Minimally Invasive Procedures
    • Arthroscopy: We perform minimally invasive arthroscopic procedures for the diagnosis and treatment of joint issues, which offer faster recovery times and reduced post-operative pain.
    • Endoscopic Surgery: This technique allows for less invasive treatment of spine and joint problems, minimizing surgical trauma and promoting quicker recovery.
  • Rehabilitation and Post-Operative Care
    • Physical Therapy: Our comprehensive rehabilitation programs are designed to help patients regain strength, flexibility, and mobility after surgery or injury.
    • Pain Management: We employ a variety of pain management strategies, including medication, physical therapy, and surgeries to ensure patient comfort throughout the recovery process.
Leadership and Expertise

Dr. Sachin Zambare: Dr. Sachin Zambare brings extensive experience and specialized training to our centre. His commitment to excellence and patient care ensures that every patient receives the highest standard of treatment.

  • Expertise in Trauma Care: Dr. Zambare is highly skilled in managing complex trauma cases, including multi-limb injuries and severe fractures. His expertise ensures that patients receive precise and effective treatment for their injuries.
  • Innovative Surgical Techniques: Under Dr. Zambare's leadership, our team employs the latest surgical techniques and technologies to enhance patient outcomes. This includes minimally invasive procedures that reduce recovery times and improve overall results.
Our Commitment to Patients

At Sterling Orthopaedic and IVF Centre, we are dedicated to providing compassionate, comprehensive care to our patients. Our approach is holistic, addressing not only the physical aspects of injury but also the emotional and psychological well-being of our patients. We strive to create a supportive and healing environment where patients feel cared for and valued.

  • Personalized Care Plans: Each patient receives a tailored treatment plan that considers their unique needs and medical history. This personalized approach ensures the best possible outcomes for our patients.
Advanced Facilities

Our centre is equipped with modern facilities and the latest medical technology to support our orthopaedic and trauma care services. This includes:

  • State-of-the-Art Operation Theatres: Designed to accommodate a wide range of surgical procedures, our operation theatres are equipped with advanced surgical instruments and technology.
  • Imaging and Diagnostic Services: We offer comprehensive imaging and diagnostic services, including high-resolution X-rays and orthoscannograms, to accurately diagnose and plan treatment for orthopaedic conditions.
