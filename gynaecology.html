Gynaecology Treatment Services

At Sterling Orthopaedic and Ivf center, we offer comprehensive gynaecological care including routine check-ups diagnostic services, and specialized treatments for a wide range of conditions affecting women's reproductive health. Our experienced team is dedicated to providing personalized and compassionate care.

Our team of gynaecologists is highly experienced and committed to providing the best possible care to our patients. We ensure that each patient receives personalized treatment and support throughout their journey to better health.

Routine Gynecological Exams

Regular health check-ups for early detection and prevention of gynecological issues. Our expert gynecologists perform thorough examinations and provide personalized care, including Pap smears, breast exams, and pelvic exams, to ensure optimal health and address all individual concerns.

  • Annual pelvic examinations
  • Pap smears and HPV testing
  • Breast examinations
Gynaecological Surgery

We offer minimally invasive gynecological surgeries for fibroids, cysts, and endometriosis, including laparoscopy, and hysteroscopy.

  • Minimally invasive laparoscopic surgery
  • Hysterectomy
  • Myomectomy (fibroid removal)
  • Ovarian cyst removal
  • Endometriosis treatment
  • Hysteroscopy for removal of polyps, septum, adhesions
  • Endometrial biopsy
Menstrual Disorders Treatment

Menstrual disorders, such as irregular periods and severe cramps, can impact daily life. Our treatment plans include lifestyle changes, medications, and surgical options tailored to each patient’s needs, aiming to manage symptoms effectively and improve quality of life.

  • Diagnosis and management of irregular periods
  • Treatment for heavy menstrual bleeding (menorrhagia)
  • Painful periods (dysmenorrhea) treatment
PCOS clinic

Our PCOS clinic provides comprehensive care and support for women with polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS). Our experienced gynaecologists offer personalized treatment plans, including hormone management, lifestyle adjustments, fertility guidance, and emotional support, to manage symptoms effectively and improve overall well-being.

  • Diagnosis and management of PCOS
Menopause Management

Comprehensive care and support for women going through menopause. Our experienced gynaecologists provide personalized treatment plans to manage symptoms and improve overall well-being during this life stage.

  • Hormone replacement therapy (HRT)
  • Management of menopausal symptoms
  • Osteoporosis screening and treatment
Adolescent Gynecology

Our adolescent gynecology services offer care for menstrual irregularities, puberty-related issues, and reproductive health education, ensuring a smooth transition through adolescence and promoting overall well-being.

  • Education on reproductive health
  • Management of menstrual disorders
  • HPV vaccination
  • Managing PCOS
Gynecological Cancer Screening and Treatment

We offer comprehensive screening and treatment for gynecological cancers, utilizing advanced diagnostic tools and personalized care plans. Our services include cutting-edge treatments and support to ensure the best possible outcomes for our patients.

  • Screening for cervical, ovarian, and uterine cancers
  • Colposcopy and biopsy
  • Treatment planning and management
Contraception and Family Planning

Our contraception and family planning services provide personalized care, offering a range of contraceptive options and family planning advice. We work with you to develop a plan that fits your lifestyle and reproductive goals, ensuring informed and confident decision-making.

  • Birth control counseling and prescriptions
  • IUD insertion and removal
